Rutgers University Fact Book

The Rutgers University Fact Book provides a ready and comprehensive source of information about Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey. It contains historical and current data about students, faculty, finances, facilities, and instruction.

The Rutgers University Fact Book is available in PDF file format. If the specific data that you are looking for has not yet been posted in this version of the Fact Book, please contact our office to see if the data needed is availabe in another format.
