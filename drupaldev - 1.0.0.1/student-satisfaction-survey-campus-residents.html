Student Satisfaction Survey for On-Campus Residents

Rutgers-New Brunswick's Residence Life participates in the Student Satisfaction Survey for On-Campus Residents Association of College and University Housing Officers International (ACUHO-I) and EBI Benchmarking Assessments each year during the Fall semester. The survey is conducted to assess student learning outcomes and satisfaction for undergraduate and graduate student residents at Rutgers along with other participating universities across the country.

To learn more about the survey please refer to ACUHO-I/EBI Assessments. For more information about Rutgers' involvement, please contact Debbie Francisco at debbie.francisco@rutgers.edu.
